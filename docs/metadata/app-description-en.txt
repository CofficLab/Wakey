Wakey - Focused Pomodoro Timer for Deep Work

A minimalist Pomodoro and focus timer for creators, developers, and knowledge workers. Designed for distraction-free sessions on macOS.

Wakey helps you structure your day into focused sprints and intentional breaks. No feeds, no social features—just a calm, reliable timer that gets out of your way.

Key Features
• Pomodoro-style focus sessions with short and long breaks
• Subtle, non-intrusive notifications at session end
• Simple, clean interface that feels native on macOS
• Lightweight design with minimal resource usage

Why Choose Wakey?

Many productivity apps try to do everything—task management, calendars, habit tracking—and end up overwhelming you. Wakey focuses on one thing only: helping you stay in deep work with a clear, visible timer and a simple flow.

Perfect For
• Developers and designers who need long, uninterrupted focus time
• Students preparing for exams or working on projects
• Writers, researchers, and creators
• Anyone who prefers a quiet, non-gamified productivity tool
